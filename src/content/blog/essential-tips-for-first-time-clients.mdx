Visiting a professional studio for the first time can feel exciting and a little intimidating. At **Open Shore Studio**, we make the process easy and enjoyable.

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your session.

## 1. Communicate Your Vision

Before your shoot, share your ideas with us. This could include:

- Mood boards  
- Reference photos  
- Social media examples  
- Color or lighting preferences  

This helps our team prepare the right lighting setup and creative direction.

## 2. Arrive Prepared

Preparation makes a huge difference in the quality of your session.

We recommend bringing:

- Multiple outfit options  
- Accessories or props  
- Backup shoes or styling items  

You can also request **hair and makeup services** before the shoot.

## 3. Trust the Process

If you’re not used to being in front of a camera, don’t worry.

Our team will guide you through:

- Poses  
- Expressions  
- Camera angles  

Relax and let us capture your best look.

## 4. Plan Enough Time

Great photography takes time. Avoid rushing your session so we can experiment with different lighting, poses, and outfit changes.

This usually leads to much better results.

## 5. Understand Your Deliverables

Before the shoot, make sure you know what your package includes, such as:

- Number of edited photos  
- Video deliverables  
- Turnaround time  
- Usage rights  

Clear expectations ensure a smooth experience.
